Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Rize subscriptions are billed through your Apple ID. To manage or cancel, open the Settings app on your iPhone, tap your name at the top, then tap Subscriptions. Select Rize and choose to cancel or change your plan. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.
How do I delete my account?
Open Rize, go to your Profile tab, and tap Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and associated data. You can also email us and we'll help you with the process.
What does the subscription include?
A Rize subscription unlocks unlimited AI Coach messages, personalized task plans, business performance tracking, and AI tools tailored to your business.
I'm having a technical issue or found a bug.
Please email rizeapphelp@gmail.com with a description of what happened and, if possible, your device model and iOS version. We'll get it sorted as quickly as we can.
